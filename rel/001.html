28 selfcomp label (or directory, or collections...)
29 holds a collection of self-compilations,
30 i.e. collections of pieces made by the authors themselves
31

33 ~~~ about release ./001 ~~~
34 
35 i started digging for stuff, to find new/recent/ongoing art.
36 Looking mainly for music, too many good pieces came to me by
37 people I wanted to hear from
38 
39 please understand that these albums are the result of dedicated
40 lives, listen carefully, comment here or in private messages about
41 the pieces and collections
42 
43 AFAIK, the time and attention of the authors are devoted
44 to finding the best in art and delivering the best art they can
45 to themselves and you,
46 often with care, kindness and good will

49 ~~~ more about selfcomp and ./001 ~~~
50 
51 selfcomp started by looking for individual summaries,
52 for lifelong collections of media
53 
54 ideally it still is, but all artist have their phases and goals,
55 and whatever they deliver to me i absorb.
56 I ask and try to keep the compilations cognizable
57 in a timespan between 10 and 60 minutes
58 
59 also looking for texts, images, videos,
60 and receiving multimedia links,
61 self-compilations of other media are being gathered
62 
63 Glerm and Antunes albums are unbelievable,
64 Machado and Basbaum albums are amusing,
65 Martigli and Gularte albumns are unanticipated
